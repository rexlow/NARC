PFMate Direct


PFMate communicates with your PF IR receiver, and can control PF motors from NXT.


Inputs
Connection- This is where you input the Input Port your PFMate is attached to.
PFMate Address- This is where you specify the address of your PFMate factory default is 48.
Operate_on-Specify which motors you will operate on, (Motor A, Motor B, or Both).
Channel-Specify the channel your PF IR receiver is tuned to.
Mode A-Specify the mode for Motor A (Run, Float or Brake).
Direction A-Specify the direction for Motor A (Forward or Reverse).
Mode B-Specify the mode for Motor B (Run, Float or Brake).
Direction B-Specify the direction for Motor B (Forward or Reverse).
Power A-Specify the Power setting for your motor A (ranging between 1 and 7)
Power B-Specify the Power setting for your motor B (ranging between 1 and 7 )
